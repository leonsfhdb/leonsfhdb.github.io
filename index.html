The Knight's Trial: Quest for the Blade

DESCRIPTION

The Knight's Trial: Quest for the Blade (or TKT:QFTB) is an exciting 2D platformer created by two passionate high school students. In this game, you play as a knight on a quest to obtain the Legendary Sword. To unlock it, you'll need to complete a series of challenging levels filled with obstacles, enemies, and puzzles. This is the full game, we made it hard on purpose, don't worry! We can't wait for you to join us on this journey! If you find any issues or bugs, let us know, you can contact us via Discord, just send a friend request to this username: leo.nsfhdb!
